What is UNOVI?

Our unique pricing model gives you an opportunity to spend 45% less money in comparison with other competitive services. Because you have no obliged payment for each user who works with the system. Pricing model uniqueness is that there are no fixed plans or costs for the system's resources. You pay only for those products, services and resources you have used. Typically the average expenses is around £27 per month. This price includes all major features. In fact you can use UNOVI forever free in case you consume resources less than allowed.
